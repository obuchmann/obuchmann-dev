This blog is where I think out loud.

Most posts mostly fall into one of two categories: ideas — half-formed theses I put up for discussion, and observations — patterns I notice in my work as a software architect and consultant. Neither comes with a claim to being correct or complete. I'm not trying to form your opinion; I'm trying to sharpen mine, and the fastest way to do that is in public.

If something here resonates — or especially if it doesn't — I'd genuinely like to hear it.
