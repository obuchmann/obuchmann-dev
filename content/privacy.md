This site sets no cookies and I store no personal data.

**Hosting.** The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). When you visit, GitHub may process technical data such as your IP address in server logs. See the [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

**Analytics.** Page views are counted with [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/), a privacy-friendly service that uses no cookies and collects no personally identifiable information. See the [GoatCounter privacy policy](https://www.goatcounter.com/help/privacy).
